<h4><strong>CCA Information</strong></h4>
<p>The Art Club caters to students who are interested in art and craft. It encourages students to express their creativity and art ideas in their art making processes, by drawing from their experiences and external resources to create their artwork.</p>
<img style="width: 75%;" src="/images/Art2.jpeg"><br>
<p>
Different art forms are introduced to develop students in different art genres. Art Club also provides opportunities for students to display school values, develop their socio-emotional learning (SEL) competencies and nurture them into confident 21st century young artists.</p>
