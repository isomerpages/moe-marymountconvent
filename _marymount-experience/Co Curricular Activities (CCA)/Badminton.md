<p>The Badminton CCA provides students with the opportunity to learn basic badminton skills such as net drop, lift, clear, smash and footwork. Badminton is a technical sport that requires a great amount of flexibility, agility, responsiveness and hand-eye coordination. As part of the gameplay, students will learn to play in modified matches in singles and doubles.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>Students with no experience or with some background training in badminton are welcome to join. Students who are able to play skillfully and competitively will be encouraged to participate in National School Games.</p><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/badminton.jpeg"></div><p></p>
