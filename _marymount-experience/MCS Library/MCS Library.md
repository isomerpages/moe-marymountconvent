<p><strong>Vision Statement</strong>
</p>
<p>Ladies of Eloquence and Intellect with God as Our Shepherd</p>
<p><strong>Mission Statement</strong>
</p>
<p>Eloquence and Intellect to Empower Lives</p>
<p><strong>Have a tour of our library:&nbsp;</strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/mcslibvideos" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/mcslibvideos</a>
</p>
<p><strong>The objectives of the School Library are:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To serve the needs of pupils through print and non-print materials like
books, reference books, magazines, digital devices and more!</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To inculcate in our pupils a life-long interest and love for reading</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To build a culture of reading in our school, for both leisure and academic
purposes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To teach good library skills that aid in our pupils’ development in media
literacy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To provide relevant resources and activities to supplement teaching and
learning in class/school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To collaborate with NLB, READ@Academy &amp; vendors to increase the pool
of teaching &amp; learning resources</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Library Dos and Don'ts</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/lib5.jpg">
</div>
<p>To make the library a quiet and pleasant place, we need all pupils to
help observe the following rules:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Speak softly at all times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Walk slowly, do not run</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not eat or drink</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not play</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Keep the library clean and tidy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take care of all library items</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Return books to the original shelves or leave them in the trolley after
reading</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Borrow the books at the borrowing station before taking them out of the
library</p>
</li>
</ul>
